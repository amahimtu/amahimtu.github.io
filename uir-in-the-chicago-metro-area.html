University-Industry Relations in the Chicago Metro Area (Data Analysis)

Communication & Media Capstone Project, University of Michigan (2019)


Overview.

As a Communication and Media capstone project, I reviewed grey literature and employed Microsoft Excel to perform a 3,000-word structured (quantitative) and unstructured (qualitative) analysis, and data visualization work on original data pertaining to university-industry relations activities in the Chicago metro area.

My analysis addressed the ways in which R&D-intensive universities (which extensively produce high-tech inventions, like advanced digital innovations) contribute to their local economies and communities, and the roles of bridge-unites and professionals facilitating it.

The project directly aided and informed the PI, Dr. Muzammil Hussain, on policy advisory research engagements for the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP) in December 2019.
